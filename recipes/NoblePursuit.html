Noble Pursuit

Description

A prized beverage in Gerudo

Ingredients

  • Alcohol
  • Cream
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Procure the ice from the fridge near Yiga base. Be sure to be swift as the ice melts quickly in the Gerudo sun
  2. In the finest glass, pour in the cream and alcohol.
  3. Finally, add the precious ice
  4. Humm a classic tune and jump for joy when complete!
